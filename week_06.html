AUTODESK FUSION 360 | Chess piece

This week lesson, we are learning how to revolve a traced image and make a piece of object with certain thickness.

Exercise 11

Attempt 1

1. Choose insert > canvas

2. Place image on y-axis

3. Trace image using curve line

4. Select surface and revolve

5. Printed using ultimaker 2+ machine. My model are too small, hence the support part sticks on the model itself, hence the interior of the piece are in solid grid style

Attempt 2

1. Scale the canvas to required dimensions and trace out the chess piece

2. Revolve surface

3. Shell the object by 2mm thk, click section analysis under inspect tool

4. Export to .3MF files

5. Open Cura app and arrange all model, make it to the centre of the machine

6. Select preset printers, I booked reality ender-3 machine 1

7. Change printing settings. Change built plate adhesion to brim instead of skirt

8. Slice model (preview) & save model to removable disk, then reject

9. Final piece
